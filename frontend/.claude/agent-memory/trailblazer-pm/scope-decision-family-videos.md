Assignments A3 (Grandma Juliaa), A6 (Grandma Vera/Grandpa Tolia), and A9 (Aunt Katiia) reference local video files that were never delivered. The GO-LIVE-CHECKLIST had a deadline of April 3 for receiving these videos.

Decision: These assignments were excluded from the active WORD_PATH and NUMBER_PATH playlists. They remain in config as dead data. The game was restructured to use progressive disclosure assignments with GDrive-hosted fun videos instead.

**Why:** Videos from family members in Italy/Ukraine were not received in time. Rather than block on content, the game pivoted to self-sourced video clues.
**How to apply:** Do not re-add these assignments unless the family videos are actually received and placed in public/assets/videos/.
